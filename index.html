What I’m Looking Forward To

Thanksgiving is a top 3 holiday and has been around since 1621. It's during a good time of year when the weather is great. My family from all over comes together, and I can’t wait to enjoy some turkey and play games in the backyard.

Why I’m Excited

The food is the highlight! My dad, uncle, and I fry the turkey, a recipe perfected over generations. I also love watching the movie *Freebird*, the ultimate Thanksgiving movie about taking turkey off the menu.

How Others Can Get Involved

Help your family cook instead of being a bystander! Talk to your family, watch *Freebird*, and enjoy the food together. Make this Thanksgiving memorable by fully participating in the festivities.
